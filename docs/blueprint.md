# **App Name**: Amana Health

## Core Features:

- Appointment Scheduling: Patients can book appointments and sync them with their calendar.
- Medical History: Allow patients to securely manage their profile and medical history.
- Secure Messaging: Enable secure communication between patients and doctors.
- Teleconsultation: Integrate video call functionality for remote consultations.
- Intelligent Diagnostic Suggestions: Leverage AI to provide tool for possible diagnosis and treatment plans based on patient symptoms and medical history.
- Automated Billing and Invoicing: Generate and send invoices to patients, process payments, and provide analytics.
- Role-Based Access Control: Implement role-based permissions to ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive data. Compliance with GDPR/HIPAA.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky Blue (#87CEEB), reflecting health, trust, and tranquility.
- Background color: Very light cyan (#E0FFFF), providing a clean and calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Lavender (#E6E6FA), adding a touch of elegance and signaling important interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean and modern look.
- Use clear and intuitive icons, with a focus on medical and calendar themes.
- A clean and user-friendly layout with easy navigation is important
- Use subtle animations to provide feedback, especially during appointment booking and confirmation.
